About

Welcome to the Sigma Alpha Mu-Gamma Rho chapter website. The Gamma Rho Chapter of UCSD is one of the oldest fraternities on UCSD's campus, celebrating its 30th year on campus in 2016.

Sammy isn't your typical fraternity; it fosters the growth and development of a diverse group of individuals who are committed to forging strong bonds of brotherhood.

We are proud of our fraternity and we are proud of the brothers who represent it. You are welcome to check back on this website anytime as we will be posting updates throughout the year. If you are interested in joining our brotherhood please feel free to come out to our rush in Spring 2016.

Philanthropy

The chapter of Sigma Alpha Mu at UCSD has a strong passion for philanthropy and service

One can regularly find brothers regularly helping at the Triton Food Pantry on campus, at various service events such as beach cleanups, and participating in various sorority philanthropies.

Our national philanthropy is The Judy Fund, a charity that is part of the Alzheimer's Association. Sigma Alpha Mu chapters across the nation raise over $30,000 for the Judy Fund.

The Gamma Rho Chapter at UCSD hosts an event called "Sammy Sandcastles," an all day event at the beach that raised over $2000 for The Judy Fund.
